$$P_2 = \frac{1}{2}\cdot P_0 + \frac{1}{2}\cdot P_3$$

$$P_3 = \frac{1}{2}\cdot P_1 + \frac{1}{2}\cdot P_4$$

$$P_4 = \frac{1}{2}\cdot P_2 + \frac{1}{3}\cdot P_5$$

Solving all these equations, we obtain the answer of \(P_3 = \frac{3}{5}\).
