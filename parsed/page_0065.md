Let A be a n × n matrix with two distinct eigenvalues λ 1 = λ 2 . Let E λ 1 and E λ 2 be the eigenspaces corresponding to the two eigenvalues. Find the cardinality ("size") of E λ 1 ∩ E λ 2 . If this intersection is not finite, enter -1 .
