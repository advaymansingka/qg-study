## 1074 Repetitious Game II

Company : SIG, Citadel

## Prompt:

Audrey repeatedly draws cards from a standard 52-card deck with replacement. Compute the expected number of draws for Audrey to get at least one card from each suit.

## Explanation:

Let X 1 denote the number of draws it takes for Audrey to draw a card from a unique suit. Let X 2 denote the number of draws it takes for Audrey to draw a card from a second unique suit after a card from the first unique suit has been draw. We'll define X 3 and X 4 in a similar way for the third and fourth unique suit draws, respectively. Then, the number of total draws it takes for Audrey to get at least one card from each suit is Z = X 1 + X 2 + X 3 + X 4 . By the Linearity of Expectation,
