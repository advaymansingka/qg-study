## Explanation:

The number of years before extinction follows a geometric distribution where p = 0 . 1 . The expected number of years before extinction is thus 1 p = 10 . This can also be solved for analytically. There is a 1 10 probability that the species will only last one year, and a 9 10 probability that the species will extend its expected number of years by one. Hence:
