## Prompt:

Your frying pan can hold up to three slices of bread for toasting. It takes one minute to toast one side of bread, and both sides of a slice of bread must be toasted in order for it to be considered toasted. Assuming that the time required to flip a slice of bread is negligible, how many minutes will it take you to toast four slices of bread?

## Explanation:

Denote the four slices of bread A,B,C,D and their sides A 1 , A 2 , B 1 , B 2 , ..., D 2 . For the first minute, you will toast A 1 , B 1 , and C 1 . For the second minute, you will toast A 2 , B 2 and D 1 . For the third and final minute, you will toast C 2 and D 2 . More generally, if M ( x, y ) is the number of minutes it takes to toast x slices of bread on a pan that fits y slices at once, then:

$$M ( x , y ) = \lceil \frac { 2 x } { y } \rceil \forall x \geq 2 , \forall y \geq 3$$
