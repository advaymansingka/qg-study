## Explanation:

We know that the ∆ for OTM options near-expiry is close to 0 and so is the Γ . The charm represents the decay in ∆ . The -0 . 04 represents that the delta is already very close to 0 as if nothing were to happen in the next hour, then the ∆ would decrease by 0 . 04 to 0 . This means that we can approximate the ∆ = 0 . 04 as we are dealing with an extremely short expiry.

Since the Γ is approximately 0 , we are moving further away from the strike and so the option price should decrease by 0 . 04 . This gives a price of 0 . 26 .
