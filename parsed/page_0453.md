## 765 Whole Lotta Dice

## Prompt:

We have 100 fair 100 -sided dice in front of us and we toss them all at the same time. What is the probability that the resulting sum of the faces is divisible by 100 ?

## Explanation:

No matter what the sum of any 99 of the dice, there is only one face on the 100 th die that will yield a sum that's divisible by 100 . For example, say the first 99 rolls add up to 3075 . Only rolling a 25 on the last die will make the sum divisible by 100 . Thus the answer is 1 100 .
