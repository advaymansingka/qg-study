The last term comes from the quadratic variation of W t . This is a martingale exactly when there is no dt term i.e. when f t ( t, w ) = -1 2 f ww ( t, w ) . We can quickly calculate that f t ( t, w ) = -cw and that f ww ( t, w ) = 6 w . Therefore, -cw = -1 2 · 6 w = -3 w , which means c = 3 .
